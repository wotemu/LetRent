About us




LetRent is a website designed by three Aalto University Computer science students. The website is developed as a requirement for the fulfillment of the course www Design.

On the website, Aalto University and other people who live in Helsinki area can post their stuffs to rent it out or sell to other communities. Hence, using the service will enable students to earn money by renting out their unused stuffs to other students or to save money by renting stuffs with other students or local communities.
